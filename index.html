About Prisma

Precision meets purpose.

Prisma Provider is a fully digital immigration document preparation company built around trust, organization and a more human client experience.

Founded by Leandro Krauss and Jessica Wisniewski, Prisma helps clients navigate USCIS form preparation with clear communication, careful organization and dependable support.

01

Our vision

To make immigration form preparation feel more accessible, personal and hopeful.

02

Our mission

To provide clear, reliable support with empathy and attention to detail.
